Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Our Commitment to Privacy

At NexusTrust Bank, we are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy.

Data Encryption

All your data is encrypted using 256-bit SSL encryption

RBI Compliant

We follow all RBI guidelines for data protection

No Tracking

We don't sell or share your personal information

Transparent Policy

Clear and simple privacy practices

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information
  • Full name and date of birth
  • Email address and phone number
  • Residential address
  • Aadhaar number (with your consent)
  • PAN card number
  • Occupation and income details
1.2 Transaction Information
  • Account balance and transaction history
  • Beneficiary details
  • IP address and device information
  • Login timestamps and activity logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain banking services
  • To process transactions and send confirmations
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send important updates and security alerts

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information:

  • With regulatory authorities as required by law
  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers for banking operations
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activities

4. Data Security

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and employee training
  • 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activities
  • Secure data centers with biometric access

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. These are small text files stored on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some features.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account closure and data removal
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact our Grievance Officer.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active and for 8 years after account closure as required by RBI regulations. After this period, your data is securely deleted.
